Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know

Getting Started

The application form takes about 10 minutes to complete. Once submitted, verification typically takes 3-5 business days. You'll be notified by email at each stage.

There are no upfront costs to join Urbance. We charge a 15% platform fee on completed jobs only. Background check costs are covered by us.

We recommend having your own liability insurance, but if you don't, we can provide coverage through our partner insurers at competitive rates.

Payments & Fees

You get paid via direct deposit within 24 hours of job completion. Customers pay upfront, and funds are released to you after the job is confirmed complete.

We charge 15% of the job total. This covers payment processing, insurance, customer support, marketing, and platform maintenance. You set your base rate, and customers see the total price including our fee.

Yes! You set your hourly or flat rate based on your experience and market rates. We provide pricing guidance based on category and location.

Working on the Platform

No, you have complete freedom to accept or decline jobs based on your schedule, location preference, and workload. There's no penalty for declining.

If a customer cancels within 24 hours of the scheduled time, you receive a cancellation fee (50% of the job value). Last-minute cancellations (less than 2 hours) are compensated at 75%.

Customers rate you on a 5-star scale after each job. Maintaining a 4.5+ average ensures priority job access and higher visibility to customers.

Support & Resources

We offer 24/7 provider support via phone, email, and in-app chat. You also get access to our knowledge base, training materials, and dedicated account manager.

Yes! All new providers complete platform training (2-3 hours). We also offer ongoing courses on customer service, business development, and technical skills.

Our support team mediates all disputes fairly. We review job details, communication records, and photos. Your payment is protected, and we work to resolve issues quickly.
